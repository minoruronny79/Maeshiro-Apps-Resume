About

A builder who's equally at home shaping strategy and prototyping with data.

A professional passionate about innovating and building things — whether as a product manager or a data scientist.

I built my foundation in financial services. As VP of Credit Risk at a Bolivian microfinance institution, I led 120+ people, grew a lending portfolio to $26M (+45%), and redesigned products that cut defaults by more than half for underserved borrowers.

I then spent years as a risk & analytics consultant, delivering credit-scoring and compliance tools to 15+ financial institutions and designing a national over-indebtedness model for a country's financial regulator.

At Oracle, I moved into product — leading roadmaps for 5+ AI/ML products spanning LLM/agent evaluation, MLOps, and AI governance, while building Python prototypes, data pipelines, and financial models myself.

What ties it together isn't one domain — it's the shape of the problem: ambiguous data, real consequences, and a system that has to earn trust. I've learned to apply that thinking across different domains: banking and investment, and the AI infrastructure sector (evaluation, governance, AgentOps, and more). Below are examples of full applications I've built across credit risk, investing, and AI agents — working tools, not slideware.
